            <h2 id="flag-raising">Flag Raising Ceremony</h2>
            <p>The astronauts planted the Lunar Flag Assembly, containing a flag of the United States, on the lunar surface in clear view of the TV camera. Aldrin remembered, "Of all the jobs I had to do on the Moon, the one I wanted to go the smoothest was the flag raising." However, the astronauts struggled with the telescoping rod and could only jam the pole about 2 inches (5 cm) into the hard lunar surface. Aldrin was concerned it might topple in front of TV viewers, but he gave "a crisp West Point salute."</p>
